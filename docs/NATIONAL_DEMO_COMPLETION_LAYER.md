# Iraq Digital Gateway (IDG)
## National Demonstration Layer: Architectural Blueprint & Verification Manual

**Target Audience:** MSC Cabinet, Ministers of Finance/Interior, GCA, CBI, NIC

### I. Executive System Summary
The Iraq Digital Gateway (IDG) National Demonstration Layer is an interactive, double-localized (Kurdish Sorani & Arabic) simulation environment built to demonstrate the economic, operational, and regulatory value of the platform in brief, high-impact presentation sessions (5–10 minutes).

The layer is built entirely inside the main web runtime to allow immediate click-and-run execution on presentation monitors during ministerial conferences, exhibitions, and senior cabinet reviews.

---

### II. Scenario Implementation Details

#### Scenario 1: AI Customs Import Assistant (Full Version)
Demonstrates the full life of an import transaction through a 7-step wizard:
1. **Importer Info**: Displays Al-Mansoori Tech's verified commercial profile, credentials, and gold status, showing zero anomalies.
2. **Cargo Info**: Details a shipment of 2,500 NextGen Hydro-Laptops worth $750,000, destined for the Zakho - Ibrahim Khalil border intersection.
3. **AI HS Code Classification**: Sugests the official tariff code `8471.30.00` (98.4% Confidence), displays full semantic processing reasoning, offers alternative HS codes, and anchors matching legislative references.
4. **Interactive Tariff Simulator**: Allows ministers to actively slide four independent tax/duty inputs:
   - **Customs Duty** (0% to 30%)
   - **VAT** (0% to 20%)
   - **Luxury Tax** (0% to 50%)
   - **Federal Administrative Fee** (0% to 10%)
   which instantly sum the total tax due on a $750k shipment.
5. **Compliance Validation**: Runs a visual Risk Engine detailing Low Risk, and provides a green checklists for all requested physical & technical commercial licenses.
6. **Logistics Pipeline**: Tracks the route visually through three transit checkpoints (Turkey Origin → Zakho Border → Baghdad Distribution Center).
7. **Executive Clearance Summary**: Displays an official certificate with Print, PDF Export, and Snapshot archiving actions.

#### Scenario 2: National Compliance Investigation (USD 1.2M Transfer)
Demonstrates high-impact AML detection through an interactive investigation deck:
- **Pricing Variance**: Highlights an invoice value of $1,200,000 against a global market value of $420,000 (representing a 185.7% price turgidity / discrepancy).
- **Compliance Flags**: Initiates automatic AML checks, flagging the transaction as *Highly Suspicious* due to overvaluation, while certifying that there are zero cross-verification matches with any domestic or international military and political sanction listings.
- **Milestone Progress**: Outlines a vertical timeline detailing five critical investigative review events (Submission of documents → Autonomous check → Flag detection → Human compliance briefing → Supreme decision).
- **Supreme Board Controls**: Offers interactive buttons for Ministers to choose either **BLOCKED (حظر)** or **APPROVED (موافق)**, which immediately updates status certificates, legal citations (Article 56 of the CBI Act), and output reports.

#### Scenario 3: National Knowledge Brain
Demonstrates the platform's legislative search and semantic reconciliation:
- **Inquiry Portal**: Allows users to type custom queries or click on pre-formed complex, critical political inquiries:
  - *"Are hybrid vehicles exempt from customs duties?"* (answering with the historic 50% tariff discount decree from the Council of Ministers in 2025).
  - *"What documents are required for importing medical equipment?"* (recalling Ministry of Health / KIMADIA ISO registration mandates).
- **Citations System**: Dynamically maps legal references, complete with governing authority, publication date, and computed trust ratings.

#### Scenario 4: National Executive Command Center
Demonstrates executive oversight through complex control screens:
- **Operational Readiness**: Features a dynamic Operational Readiness Score (98% baseline) constructed out of security, performance, compliance, and reliability criteria.
- **Incident Severity Filters**: Groups active security alerts into severe categories (Critical, Warning, Info) with instant filter buttons.
- **Dynamic Threat Scenarios**: Allows presenters to toggle threats (Normal, High, Critical) and watch the dashboard metrics and readiness score animate to reflect the change.

### III. Dual-Localization Compliance
- **Sorani Kurdish**: Custom-tailored to represent the formal terminology of cabinet communications in official ministries.
- **Arabic**: Formulated in formal federal state language, conforming to the Iraqi Ministry of Finance and General Customs Authority's official communication standards.
- **100% Excluded English**: Every trace of English text has been cleared from charts, tables, cards, tooltips, buttons, metrics, and error labels. Instantly toggleable between both languages.
